Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our pressure washing services

How much does pressure washing cost?

Our packages start at $39 for vehicle washing and $149 for residential driveways and patios. Commercial pricing varies by project size. All quotes are free!

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! All estimates are completely free with no obligation. We'll assess your property and provide transparent, upfront pricing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. HydroHogs is fully licensed and carries comprehensive insurance for your complete peace of mind.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Amelia Island, Fernandina Beach, Yulee, Callahan, and surrounding Nassau County areas.

Do you use eco-friendly products?

Yes! We use environmentally safe cleaning solutions that are effective on dirt and grime but gentle on your landscape and family.

How long does a typical job take?

Most residential jobs take 2-4 hours depending on the package. Commercial projects vary by scope. We'll provide estimated timeframes with your quote.
